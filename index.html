    Welcome to TKSPC

    Theni Kammavar Sangam Polytechnic College (TKSPC), is a co-educational, self financed institution, founded & governed by Theni Kammavar Sangam. It is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE), Tamilnadu. It follows the curriculum and evaluation system prescribed by Directorate of Technical Education and offers three years diploma programs to eligible students as per Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE) norms.

    About Us

    The philanthropists belonging to Kammavar community residing in and around Theni formed a society in the year 1972 and started a Matriculation School in the town. 25 years of their educational service they realized the necessity of offering a value based technical education to uplift the lifestyle of educationally and economically backward people in this region and started a Polytechnic college and Engineering college simultaneously in the year 2007—2008 in the name “Theni Kammavar Sangam Polytechnic College” and “Theni Kammavar Sangam College of Technology”.

    Theni Kammavar Sangam Polytechnic College

    is situated at the out-skirts of Theni on Theni—Venkatachalapuram main road at 2km from Theni—Madurai Highway. It has a built up area of 10,239 sqmts, accommodating all class rooms and all laboratories in a ground plus three floors building inside the big campus extending to 75 acres. The calm and evergreen environment provides a conducive atmosphere for learning.

    All the laboratories are fully equipped with modern equipments and practical training is given as per syllabus prescribed by Directorate of Technical Education. Also job oriented training programs in advanced specialized areas and soft skill development programs are being conducted so as to enable the students to get employment immediately after completion of diploma.

    Placement

    The placement cell at TKSPC plays an integral role in guiding the students achieve their career goals. The placement cell facilitates the training activities with the help of industry experts, alumni and corporate trainers to prepare the students industry-ready. The placement cell collaborates with some of the best organisations across industries to provide on-campus job opportunities. As the students excel in their career life, TKSPC has always been a much favoured talent hunting ground for the corporate world.

    The objective of teaching faculties is to provide students with a high-quality education by delivering instruction, designing curriculum, and assessing student progress. They aim to impart knowledge, develop skills, and foster critical thinking in their students so that they can be successful in their future careers and as informed citizens. Additionally, they often strive to create a positive and inclusive learning environment that promotes engagement, curiosity, and personal growth.
