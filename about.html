Vision

To facilitate transformation of a students into good human beings, responsible citizens and competent technicians and to get developed as centre of excellence.

Mandate

Theni Kammavar Sangam Polytechnic College (TKSPC), is a co-educational, self financed institution, founded & governed by Theni Kammavar Sangam. It is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE), Tamilnadu. It follows the curriculum and evaluation system prescribed by Directorate of Technical Education and offers three years diploma programs to eligible students as per Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE) norms.

Mission

Theni Kammavar Sangam Polytechnic College is a co-educational, self financial institution founded by Theni Kammavar Sangam with a philanthropic view to uplift the rural society. It strives to impart high quality technical education to the society by

  • Providing an environment conducive to innovative and creative thinking and learning
  • Offering need based competitive and continuous non- formal education in tantem with emerging technological development.
  • Establishing linkage with Industries, Private and Government organizations for sharing the knowledge and resources available for mutual growth and development.
  • Inculcating values and ethics in the minds of the students and people in the society to make them a holistic and better citizens of the country
