Library

The aim of a library is to provide access to information, knowledge, and resources that support education, learning, research, and personal growth. This can include books, periodicals, journals, newspapers, and other printed materials, as well as digital resources such as e-books, databases, and online journals. Libraries also provide services such as research assistance, technology access, and programming to help patrons make the most of the resources available to them. Additionally, libraries aim to promote literacy, lifelong learning, and the love of reading and knowledge. They also contribute to the preservation of cultural heritage by collecting, preserving, and providing access to historical and cultural materials.

Our focus will be on:

  • Providing access to a wide range of information and resources, including books, periodicals, journals, newspapers, and digital resources such as e-books, databases, and online journals.
  • Offering services such as research assistance, technology access, and programming to help patrons make the most of the resources available to them.
  • Promoting literacy, lifelong learning, and the love of reading and knowledge.
  • Collecting, preserving, and providing access to historical and cultural materials to contribute to the preservation of cultural heritage.
  • Creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all patrons.
  • Providing a safe and secure space for patrons to access and use resources.
  • Continuously updating and expanding its resources and services to meet the changing needs of patrons and the community.
  • Collaborating with other organizations and institutions to enhance the services it provides.
  • Being an educational and cultural center for the community.
  • Providing equal access to information for all patrons regardless of background, abilities or socio-economic status.

Hostel

College hostel for boys and girls are dormitory-style housing facilities provided by TKS - Hostel for all our group of college students. These hostels typically offer shared rooms and communal areas such as kitchens, lounges, and bathrooms. The main objective of a college hostel is to provide safe and comfortable accommodation for students who are away from home and studying at the college. College hostels for boys and girls are usually separate, as they are intended for single-gender students. The facilities, rules and regulations may be different for boys and girls hostel, depending on the college. College hostels are typically managed by the college, and students are usually required to follow certain rules and regulations to ensure the safety and well-being of all residents.

Our focus will be on:

  • Providing safe and comfortable accommodation for students who are away from home and studying at the college.
  • Offering shared dormitory-style rooms, as well as communal areas such as kitchens, lounges, and bathrooms.
  • Creating a sense of community among students, and may offer additional services such as tutoring, counseling, or recreational activities.
  • Providing meal plans and laundry services, to make the stay of students more comfortable.
  • Being managed by the college and students are required to follow certain rules and regulations to ensure the safety and well-being of all residents.
  • Have separate facilities for boys and girls.
  • Providing 24-hour security and surveillance.
  • Providing necessary infrastructure and amenities like internet, heating, air conditioning etc.
  • Providing a safe and secure space for students to study, sleep and relax.
  • Creating an environment that encourages personal growth and development.
  • Being budget-friendly and providing an affordable alternative for students who can't stay with their families.

Physical Education

Physical education (PE) is a course that focuses on the development of physical fitness and the knowledge and skills necessary for participation in various physical activities. Physical education classes usually include a combination of physical activity, such as games, sports, and fitness exercises, as well as instruction in health and nutrition. The main goal of physical education is to promote physical fitness and overall wellness in students. This includes developing strength, flexibility, endurance, and coordination, as well as teaching proper exercise technique and injury prevention. Additionally, physical education classes may also teach students about the importance of healthy eating and the impact of physical activity on overall health. Physical education is considered an essential part of a well-rounded education and is often required for graduation from high school. Our college also offer physical education courses as part of our curriculum

Our focus will be on:

  • Promoting physical fitness: Physical education classes aim to improve students' physical fitness by providing them with a variety of activities that challenge their strength, endurance, flexibility, and coordination.
  • Developing motor skills: Physical education classes focus on helping students develop the motor skills necessary for participation in various physical activities, such as running, jumping, throwing, and catching.
  • Teaching proper exercise technique: Physical education teachers instruct students on the proper technique for various exercises to help prevent injuries and ensure that students are getting the most out of their physical activity.
  • Educating on health and nutrition: Physical education classes often include instruction on the importance of healthy eating and the impact of physical activity on overall health.
  • Encouraging lifelong physical activity: Physical education classes aim to instill a love of physical activity in students and to encourage them to continue to be active throughout their lives.
  • Addressing the current health concerns: Many physical education classes now incorporate activities that promote mental and emotional well-being, such as yoga and mindfulness exercises.
  • Adapting to the learning environment: In the current scenario, many physical education classes are being conducted remotely or through hybrid learning, requiring teachers to adapt their instruction and activities accordingly.
  • Inclusivity: Physical education classes should strive to be inclusive and welcoming for all students, regardless of their physical abilities or fitness levels.

Transport

The institutions have a robust transport system with a fleet of buses operating from our various campus. They facilitate ease of conveyance for both students and staffs. The buses ply to and from the prominent parts inside the Theni city and its sub-urban areas. Bus available to Theni, Periyakulam, Bodinayakkanur, Thevaram, Cumbum and Andipatti covering a distance of 100 Kms radius. Luxurious bus for long distance areas for a comfortable travelling.

Our focus will be on:

  • Convenience: College transport options should provide a convenient and reliable way for students, staff, and faculty to get to and from campus.
  • Safety: Safety should be a top priority for college transport options. This includes ensuring that vehicles and equipment are well-maintained, and that drivers and other personnel are properly trained.
  • Accessibility: College transport options should be accessible to all students, staff, and faculty, including those with disabilities or special needs.
  • Cost-effectiveness: College transport options should be cost-effective for students, staff, and faculty. This can include offering free or discounted transportation services or providing parking facilities at a reasonable cost.
  • Sustainability: committed to promoting sustainable transportation. This can include encouraging carpooling, bike sharing, or using alternative fuel vehicles.
  • Integration with other forms of transportation: College transport options should be well-integrated with other forms of transportation, such as public transportation or bike-sharing programs, to make it easy for students, staff, and faculty to get to and from campus.
  • Security: College transport options should have measures in place to ensure the security of students, staff, and faculty while they are travelling.
  • Adapting to the current situation: In the current scenario, some transport options may need to be adapted or changed to ensure compliance with health and safety guidelines.

Canteen

A college canteen, also known as a college cafeteria, is a food service facility located on a college or university campus. The main function of a college canteen is to provide meals and snacks for students, staff, and faculty.

Our focus will be on:

  • Variety of food options: College canteens typically offer a variety of food options, including hot meals, snacks and beverages. Also offer vegetarian, vegan, or halal options.
  • Convenience: College canteens are located in convenient areas on campus, such as near residence halls or academic buildings, making it easy for students, staff, and faculty to access them.
  • Meal plans: offer meal plans for students, which allow them to purchase meals at the canteen using a prepaid account. This can be more cost-effective than paying for each meal individually.
  • Operating hours: College canteens typically have set operating hours (9am to 5pm), which may vary depending on the day of the week
  • Health and safety: Our canteen follows health and safety regulations, including regular inspections by government authorities. We should also adhere to current public health guidelines.
  • Cost-effective: College canteens often offer more affordable prices than off-campus restaurants and fast food chains, allowing students to save money on their food expenses.
